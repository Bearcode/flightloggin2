Key features of FlightLogg.in' include:
  • Web-based - Access your data from any internet enabled device
  • Email Backups - Automatically sends you a copy of all your logbook data to your email for redundancy and piece of mind. If the site ever goes down, you always have the latest copy of your logbook data in your email archives
  • Simple, no-nonsense user interface - designed for efficiency and easy of use.
  • Open Source - The code that runs FlightLogg.in' is completely open source.
  • No lock-in - Export your data at any time in a CSV format that con be read by any program.
  • Ad supported - No subscription fees.
  • Badges - Sort of like achievements for the XBox. Unlock badges for logging flights to new airports, logging long flights, and reaching flight hour milestones.
  • Create data visualization such as graphs and histograms.
  • Location Awareness - automatically calculates the distance of your flights based on the airport identifiers in the 'route' column.
