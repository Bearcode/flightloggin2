Key features of FlightLogg.in' include:
  • Web-based - Access your data from any internet enabled device
  • Email Backups - Automatically sends you a copy of all your logbook data to your email for redundantcy and piece of mind.
  • Sinple, no-nonsense user interface - designed for efficiency and easy of use
  • Open Source - The code that runs FlightLogg.in' is completely open source
  • No lock-in - Export your data at any time in a format that con be read by any program
  • Ad supported - No subscription fees
  • Create Data visualization such as graphs and histograms
  • Location Awareness - automatically calculates the diatance of your flights based on the airport identifiers in the 'route' column
